# Dashboard Review Checklist

- Can the first screen answer the dashboard's main question in a few seconds?
- Is there a compact summary band up top, with lower-priority sections collapsed or split into focused siblings?
- Is the chosen layout mode correct for this dashboard, and are rows, tabs, and repeat behavior helping instead of adding scan cost?
- Does every panel earn its place by supporting overview, diagnosis, or drill-down?
- Are the default time range, timezone behavior, refresh choice, and any `Now delay` coherent across the dashboard?
- Do annotations have a clear scope and visibility plan, or are they adding noise?
- Are dashboard links, panel links, and data links used for the right jobs with correct time and variable carryover?
- Are dropdown sibling links preserving the right variables and time window, or should they reset context?
- Do placeholder text panels or blank row titles add meaning, or are they just layout debt?
- Are titles, descriptions, units, legends, and links clear enough for someone new to the dashboard?
- Is there a simpler layout that answers the same question with fewer panels?
